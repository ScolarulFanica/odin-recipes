Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is an indian dish with a lot of spices

Ingredients

  • 60 ml of Oil
  • 1 Kg of Chicken cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 30 of ginger, minced
  • 40g of curry powder
  • 40g of Garam Masala
  • 1 carrot

Steps

  1. Add the oil to the pot on medium heat and saute the garlic until brown
  2. Add the onions and ginger and then saute the chicken pieces on all sides and let it cook
  3. Add the curry powder and garam masala
  4. Saute for 5-7 minutes and then add the carrots and a cup of water
  5. Let it simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Add salt and pepper after taste
