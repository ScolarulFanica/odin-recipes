Sarmale

Description

Sarmale is a dish specific to the Balkan region, of turkic or arab influence

Sarmale is particularly a romanian dish, a cabbage roll containing a mix of meat, usually pork or beef and rice, optionally other vegetables

Ingredients

  • 500g of Rice
  • 1 Cabbage
  • 3-4 Thyme Leaves
  • 3 Clovers of Garlic
  • 1l of Tomato Sauce
  • 500 g - Mix of beef and pork meat
  • 200g of Peppers

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the rice and then let it cool down
  2. Cook the peeled cabbage leaves for 2 hours in a pot of water on low heat
  3. Mix the rice with the mix of meats and wrap them in cabbage leaves
  4. Mince the garlic and chop the peppers and add them to a big pot
  5. After 5 minutes add the tomato sauce with 1l of water
  6. Let it simmer for 20 minutes and add the thyme leaves and the filled cabbage rolls
  7. Leave the pot on the stove for 2-3 hours on low heat
  8. Rest the pot for another 30 minutes and then enjoy the meal
